Kalakrithi 2017, is a platform for passionate dance teams to evince the indelible dancing talents of their crew. The best dance teams will qualify to encaptivate the young crowd to be crowned as the ‘Kalakrithi Dance Team of the year 2017’.

REGISTRATION

  • The teams have to present themselves at the event venue by 9A.M. for registration. All team members must be present at the time of registration.
  • Slots will be allotted on ‘First come first serve basis’.
  • Registrants should carry original institutional ID cards and a photocopy of the same should be submitted at the registration desk.
  • Tracks/audio should not contain any offensive content. Tracks should be submitted at the registration desk.(preferably in pendrive)


    • The minimum number of participants in a team should be 8 and maximum should be 30. The track limit will be 4minutes and it shouldn’t exceed beyond the limit.
    • The teams must submit the photocopy of their ID cards to the event coordinators.
    • Water, inflammable and heavy objects are not allowed to be used on stage by the participating teams.
    • No unethical behavior will be tolerated.
    • The participants should take care of their own belongings. The organizing team will not take up the responsibility of it.
    • No rooms for changing would be provided. The participants are requested to come prepared to the venue.
    • Judge’s decision will be final.
